Something about us!

This site is for the fans of beards!

The purpose of this site is to increase awareness, appreciation, and understanding of the beard. The intended audience is anyone who has any interest in or curiosity about beards. It is also intended for anyone who has an opinion about beards, whether good, bad, or neutral. Whatever your opinion on beards, I hope a visit to these pages will improve it to some degree. This site is an informal experimental project in beard research. Your feedback is welcome and is encouraged. Thanks for visiting.

We don’t hold any rights for images, videos and so on. This is contribution for the beard fans. Any sugestion, good or bad you can contact us bellow. We will be happy to reply!
